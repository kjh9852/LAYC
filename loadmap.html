Diamond

LAC's unique utility token is the ‘Diamond’.
These have no real market value outside of LAC.
Holders can earn ‘Diamonds’ through mission
and use them to purchase items through the LAC Shop.

Yacht Club

Actual real-life exclusive Yacht Party to be held in Dubai.
Details of this exclusive event will be updated at a later date.

BAYC Holder Event

100 people holding the most expensive
BAYCs will each receive 1 LAC for free.
Holders will be rewarded automatically
with Super DNA.

Super DNA

Super DNA has a higher chance
to breed high-tier Baby Apes
and this is only offered to BAYC holders.
Breeding can be done after purchasing
a Jewelry Box required for breeding.

LAC shop

A store with various content for LAC holders.
Holders can use Diamonds to purchase items in the LAC Shop.

Jewelry Box

A ‘Jewelry Box’ is required to breed Baby
Apes when used together with ‘DNA’
(similar to an incubator).
Each Jewelry Box is sold for 600 Diamond Tokens.

Key

The ‘key’ is used to open the ‘Powder Room’.
The price is yet to be determined.

DNA

A ‘DNA’ is required to breed Baby Apes
when used together with a ‘Jewelry Box’.
Each DNA is sold for 300 Diamond Tokens.

Mystery Ticket

A Mystery Ticket allows holders
to participate in various upcoming content.
The tickets can be found in the Powder Room.

Powder Room

A dressing table that contains mystery tickets.
The price is yet to be determined.

Baby Apes

Baby Apes are divided into three tiers:
Silver, Gold, and Diamond. Tier Grades
are given randomly when bred using ‘DNA’ and a ‘Jewelry Box’.
